Cycle the Continental Divide

Over 3,000 miles that cuts through the back side of Mexico thru Canada and nearly a thousand miles of it bisects the western half of Montana which is considered the longest mountain trail in the state.

Date of Event

The Essentials

In engaging in this trail, these are the things required (but not limited to) for every bikers.

  • Map
  • Compass
  • Water
  • Lightweight Food
  • Extra Clothes
  • First-Aid Kit
  • Pocket Knife
  • Sun Protection
  • Flashligt
  • Matches and Firestarter
